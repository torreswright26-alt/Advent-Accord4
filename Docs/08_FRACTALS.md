# Fractals

## Definition
Fractals are external failures of reality attempting to form bodies.

## Visual Rules
- Not manifestations.
- Not generic humanoid monsters.
- Not restricted to purple.
- May have creature-like shapes when useful, but should still feel like reality destabilizing.
- Scale may range from small to environmental.
- Their presence may distort the world around them.

## Intelligence
Higher-tier fractals may possess awareness or consciousness.
This is initially rumor, later known by upper ranks.
Some may become wardens; others become destructive.
Their moral complexity should not be revealed too early.
