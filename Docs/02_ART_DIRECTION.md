# Art Direction

## Core Identity
Advent Accord should read as a confident painterly illustration rather than a rendered 3D scene, generic anime frame, or hyper-detailed concept-art composite.

## Primary Qualities
- Painterly brush economy
- Medium-frequency detail
- Strong composition
- Large value masses
- Selective complexity
- Clear focal hierarchy
- Rich but controlled color
- Cinematic light
- Environmental storytelling
- Lived-in spaces

## What "Painterly" Means Here
- Forms are described with shape and value before texture.
- Not every surface is fully resolved.
- Backgrounds simplify with distance.
- Foliage is grouped into masses.
- Stone is suggested rather than individually engraved.
- Cloth is defined by major folds, silhouette, and light.
- Empty or quiet visual areas are allowed.
- Brush language should feel intentional rather than algorithmically noisy.

## Avoid
- Hyperrealism
- PBR material treatment
- Uniform micro-detail
- Excessive local contrast
- Dense particles, debris, dust, sparks, snow, leaves, or floating motes
- Geometric clothing artifacts
- Hard-light constructs unless canonically justified
- Plastic skin
- Excessive sharpening
- Flat generic anime rendering
- Dark palette as a default
