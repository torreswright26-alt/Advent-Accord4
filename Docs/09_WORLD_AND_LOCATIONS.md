# World and Locations

## Opening Location: Bryn
- Sol falls from a rift into an alley.
- He is frightened and amnesiac.
- The first fractal encounter follows.
- Bryn should support intimate alleys, public squares, training spaces, dormitories, markets, shops, streets, rooftops, and distant skyline views.

## Render References
The established triptych of:
- central square,
- training hall,
- dormitory
serves as a rendering benchmark.

## Location Diversity
Different locations may intentionally feel out of place for narrative reasons.
A location may possess a distinct design language without violating global render consistency.

## Environment Rule
Manifestations should integrate naturally rather than replacing architecture, geography, weather, or lighting.
