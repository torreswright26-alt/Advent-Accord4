# Story and Timeline

## Opening
- The first chapter begins with nine-year-old Sol falling from a rift into a Bryn alley.
- He is frightened and amnesiac.
- His first fractal encounter follows.

## Pacing
The first chapter was intentionally expanded to five subchapters so events can breathe.

## Narrative Rules
- Do not bounce rapidly between unrelated events.
- Do not manufacture constant lessons.
- Do not add unnecessary subchapters.
- Do not rush the day or compress emotional transitions.
- Let conversations and environmental movement carry narrative weight.
