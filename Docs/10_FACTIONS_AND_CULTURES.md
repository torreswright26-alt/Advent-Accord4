# Factions and Cultures

## Mirror Faction
- Standard masked operative is an established NPC type.
- Ranks require distinct silhouettes and role-specific uniforms.
- The faction should not be restricted to charcoal or black clothing.
- A mask, emblem, or accessory may remain consistent across ranks.
- Members should be allowed visual self-expression.
- Subterfuge is central to the faction's identity.

## Cultural Design
Use varied palettes, textiles, architecture, adornment, and silhouettes.
Avoid making every faction dark, militarized, or monochromatic.
