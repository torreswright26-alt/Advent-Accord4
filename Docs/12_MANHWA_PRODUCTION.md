# Manhwa Production

## Paneling
- Vary panel scale and camera angle.
- Avoid repeated poses and repeated directions of gaze.
- Let scenes breathe.
- Preserve spatial continuity.
- Do not rush through events.
- Keep dialogue readable and correctly assigned.
- Avoid recurring chat-bubble mistakes.
- Do not add extra subchapters unless requested.

## Character Acting
Use:
- posture,
- gesture,
- eye direction,
- spatial relationship,
- clothing movement,
- interaction with objects,
- expression progression.

## Common Sequence Failures
- repeated sitting,
- repeated looking over one shoulder,
- repeated search for a seat,
- characters standing as spectators,
- events advancing too quickly,
- repeated framing,
- dialogue doing all narrative work.

## Text
Do not add text to generated artwork unless explicitly requested.
